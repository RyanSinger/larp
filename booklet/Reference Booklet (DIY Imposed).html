Reference Booklet · DIY Imposed (fold & staple) Print-shop view →
Print this one at home, then fold & staple. Pages are imposed two-up for saddle stitch. Print on US Letter, landscape, double-sided, flip on the SHORT edge, at “Actual size” (100%, no scaling). Keep the sheets in the order they print; stack them in that order, fold the whole stack in half, and staple twice along the spine. Each sheet is labelled (front/back · page numbers) in the corner — those labels sit outside the fold and can be trimmed. Sending it to a real shop instead? Use the print-shop master.
